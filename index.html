Why am I on ClickStart


I joined the ClickStart course for a multitude of reasons;

  • Learning with other people
  • Being able to ask questions
  • Learning in person

My previous computer and IT experience is relatively general, with me having a good general grasp and not struggling
with many issues and being able to troubleshoot my own issues. I've explored tools for game engines like Unreal Engine 4
and the source engine for map making. I've also spent time with Photoshop, Adobe Premiere and the Microsoft 365 Suite.

My goal now is to build up a portfolio in UI design for websites and games to refine my UI/UX skillset, whilst I
continue to learn HTML, CSS and Javascript.
